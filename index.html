I design educations.

Humans are marvelous creatures, capable of understanding their world in ways far beyond the limits of our simple senses. Designing education is about removing the limits to our inherent capacities.

In my work as an education designer, I define and build curricula using both time-tested and modern pedagogical methods to give students access to meaningful, transformative, and persistent learning.
